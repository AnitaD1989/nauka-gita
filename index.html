Plastic can be fantastic!

Nice things made out of recycled materials

Giving plastic waste a new life is the future of the world. A whopping 91% of the world plastic isn't recycled and plays a huge role in the pollution.

Things made out of plastic waste are cool and classy

Plastic products are very common in our modern life.
Plastic does not completely decompose, it stays in the environment one way or another forever.

The best way is just to reuse it. The possibilities of creating things out of recycled plastic are endless and can solve the pollution problem, but people have to take care more of recycling the stuff and we may need new and more regulations.

What you can make out of recycled plastic?

Chairs

Chairs are useful and easy to make. Some companies offer nicely designed chairs made 100% out of plastic waste. For example dutch designer Dave Hakkens, who created "onearmy" shares lots of ideas, of how to turn plastic in furniture, jewelerry etc.
For more information visit: precious plastic

Jewellery

Stylish and unique jewellery is one of many good and creative ideas for plastic waste. I love the jewellery made by Cura jewellery. The jewellery is handcrafted in Switzerland and course made out of plastic waste.
